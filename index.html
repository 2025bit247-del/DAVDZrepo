Welcome to Mbarara Connect

Mbarara Connect is a digital platform designed to connect tourists, students, and local communities to the rich experiences found in Mbarara city.

From Lake Mburo National Park to local cultural sites and hidden food spots, this platform provides a centralized source of reliable information to help visitors explore, navigate, and enjoy Mbarara.

Our goal is to promote tourism, support local businesses, and improve access to authentic experiences in Western Uganda.
