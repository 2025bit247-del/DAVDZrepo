Project Background

Mbarara is one of Uganda’s fastest-growing cities, known for tourism, culture, and business opportunities. However, information about attractions and services is scattered and difficult to access.

Objectives

  1. Promote tourism in Mbarara
  2. Support local businesses through online visibility
  3. Improve navigation using digital tools
  4. Preserve cultural heritage
  5. Support student entrepreneurship

Key Concepts

Tourism Platform
A system that connects visitors with local attractions and services.
Digital Visibility
Making businesses discoverable online.
Cultural Heritage
Traditions and practices preserved for future generations.
